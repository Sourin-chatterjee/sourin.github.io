Biosketch

I am pursuing Ph.D. in Physics at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras. My research includes frustrated quantum magnetism and computational approaches to emergent phases, with work involving PSG classification, mean-field Ansätze, and structure-factor calculations for experimental comparison.

Interests: quantum spin liquids, frustrated lattices, renormalization-group approaches, computational modeling.

Key Projects

1) Semi-Dirac spin liquids and frustrated quantum magnetism on the trellis lattice
Ph.D. project · Guide: Prof. Yasir Iqbal · IIT Madras

  • Performed fully symmetric PSG classification using Abrikosov fermions.
  • Identified 256 Z₂ and 448 U(1) algebraic PSGs; reduced to symmetry-allowed mean-field Ansätze.
  • Studied emergent phases; computed static & dynamical structure factors for experimental comparison.
  • Paper: arXiv:2509.02663

Keywords: Quantum Spin Liquids (QSLs), Projective Symmetry Group (PSG)
